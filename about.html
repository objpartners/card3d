Meet Otis B. Jennings, PhD
Chief Art-Scientist

After years as a professor and then in corporate settings, my true passions became clear to me. I enjoy brainteasers, artistic expression, entrepreneurship, sharing knowledge, helping others, and completing projects with smart and talented friends. But these challenges usually had to wait until the workday was done.

CARD3D LLC is the vehicle that lets me accomplish all of these passions at once.

CARD3D Ornaments occurred to me years ago. And I made a few for friends. Recently, I decided to share this gifting opportunity to the world, as a way to energize the greeting card given process. There are more products on my vision board. Stay tuned...
